What is the color of leaves?
Most often, leaves are green. However, they can change their color to yellow, orange or others.
Why do leaves change color in the fall?
It is because of the sunlight. When fall comes, leaves receive less sunlight and stop producing food for the plant to prepare for the winter. The green chlorophyll fades and other colors begin to show up.
